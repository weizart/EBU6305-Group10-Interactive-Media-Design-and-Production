BeiJing

Beijing is the ancient capital of China, a historical and cultural city with a history of more than 3,000 years and a capital history of more than 860 years. It was the capital of Liao, Jin, Yuan, Ming and Qing dynasties. Beijing has a collection of modern Chinese culture since Yuan, Ming and Qing Dynasties. It has numerous historical sites and cultural landscapes, and has seven World Heritage sites, making it the city with the largest number of cultural heritage projects in the world. There are many historic sites in Beijing, including the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven, the Summer Palace, the Old Summer Palace and Beihai Park. Hutongs and Siheyuan, as typical residential forms in Beijing's old town, have become important cultural symbols in Beijing's history. Beijing is an important tourist town in China and has been awarded a "three-star Travel recommendation" (the highest level) by the Michelin Travel Guide. Beijing plays an important role in global political, economic and other social activities and has the leading and radiating ability. It ranks 6th in the GaWC Global Cities ranking, second only to Hong Kong and Shanghai compared with other Chinese cities. Beijing has been rated as a first-tier city in the world by GaWC, a world city research institute, and the United Nations reported that the Human Development Index of Beijing ranks the second among Chinese cities. Beijing is also the only city in the world to host both the Summer and Winter Olympic Games.

For tourists

Beijing has rich tourism resources, including 2 national key scenic spots, 1 national historic and cultural city, 1 Chinese historical and cultural village, 99 national key cultural relic protection units, 326 municipal cultural relic protection units, etc. The city has seven World Heritage sites, more than any other city in the world.

For investors

In recent years, Beijing's real estate and automobile industries have continued to develop. The manufacturing industry of Beijing includes automobile industry, biomedicine industry, opto-electromechanical industry, microelectronics industry and so on. In 2019, Beijing's tourism industry received 3.78 million overseas tourists and 310 million domestic tourists.

For professionals

Beijing has a significant influence in the political, economic and trade, cultural, educational and technological fields of mainland China. It is where the headquarters of most state-owned enterprises in China is located, and has the headquarters of the world's largest Fortune Global 500 enterprises. The business center of Beijing gathers a large number of international financial business office facilities and skyscrapers. Zhongguancun area is concentrated in mainland China's important high-tech industries.

Dongcheng District

The Palace Museum

Locate:No.4 Jingshan Front Street, Beijing

Open:8:30-16:30


The Palace Museum, formerly known as the Forbidden City, is the imperial palace of China's Ming and Qing dynasties. It is located at the center of the central axis of Beijing. The Palace Museum in Beijing is centered on three main halls, covering an area of about 720,000 square meters, with a building area of about 150,000 square meters. There are more than 70 palaces and more than 9,000 rooms.The Palace Museum is one of the largest and best preserved ancient wooden structures in the world. It is a national AAAAA tourist attraction.

Beihai Park

Locate:No.1 Wenjin Street, Xicheng District, Beijing

Open:6:30--20:00


Beihai Park, facing Zhongnanhai in the south and Shichahai in the north, covers an area of 682,000 square meters (389,000 square meters of water area and 293,000 square meters of land area). Haidian District

The Summer Palace

Locate:No.23, Gongmenqian Street, Haidian District, Beijing

6:00-20:00


The Summer Palace, formerly known as the Garden of Clear Springs, is located in the western suburbs of Beijing, 15 kilometers away from the city. It covers an area of 3.009 square kilometers and is adjacent to the Old Summer Palace. It is a large landscape garden built on the base of Kunming Lake and Longevity Hill, modeled on Hangzhou West Lake and drawing on the design techniques of Jiangnan gardens. It is also the most complete preserved royal palace and garden, known as the "Museum of Royal Gardens".

Beijing Garden Expo Garden

Locate:No.15 Shooting Range Road, Beijing

Open:6:30-17:30


Beijing Garden Expo Garden is located on the west bank of Yongding River, Fengtai District, north to Lianshi West Road, south to Meishikou Road west extension, west to Beigong Road, southwest to Yuanboda Road. Huairou District

Yanqi Lake

Locate:No.3 Yanshui Road, Huaibei Town, Huairou District

Open:8:00-17:30


Yanqi Lake is located at the foot of Yanshan Mountain, 8 kilometers north of Huairou City on the outskirts of Beijing. It is an area of water and land centered on the lake, with beautiful scenery and clear water. Every spring and autumn there are often groups of wild geese to roost in the lake, hence the name. The scenic spot is mainly divided into several parts: First, the core area is Yanqi Island (Yanqi Peninsula) conference center and supporting facilities (Summer Garden, Yanqi Island hotel and villa group); second, the Beijing Yanqi Lake International Convention and Exhibition Center and supporting Beijing Sunrise Oriental Kempinski Hotel (one of the Yanqi Lake International Convention and Exhibition Area); third, the natural landscape of the lake; fourth, various play areas; fifth, the lake district custom street and nearby town.
