BeiJing

Beijing is the ancient capital of China, a historical and cultural city with a history of more than 3,000 years and a capital history of more than 860 years. It was the capital of Liao, Jin, Yuan, Ming and Qing dynasties. Beijing has a collection of modern Chinese culture since Yuan, Ming and Qing Dynasties. It has numerous historical sites and cultural landscapes, and has seven World Heritage sites, making it the city with the largest number of cultural heritage projects in the world. There are many historic sites in Beijing, including the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven, the Summer Palace, the Old Summer Palace and Beihai Park. Hutongs and Siheyuan, as typical residential forms in Beijing's old town, have become important cultural symbols in Beijing's history. Beijing is an important tourist town in China and has been awarded a "three-star Travel recommendation" (the highest level) by the Michelin Travel Guide. Beijing plays an important role in global political, economic and other social activities and has the leading and radiating ability. It ranks 6th in the GaWC Global Cities ranking, second only to Hong Kong and Shanghai compared with other Chinese cities. Beijing has been rated as a first-tier city in the world by GaWC, a world city research institute, and the United Nations reported that the Human Development Index of Beijing ranks the second among Chinese cities. Beijing is also the only city in the world to host both the Summer and Winter Olympic Games.

For tourists

Beijing has rich tourism resources, including 2 national key scenic spots, 1 national historic and cultural city, 1 Chinese historical and cultural village, 99 national key cultural relic protection units, 326 municipal cultural relic protection units, etc. The city has seven World Heritage sites, more than any other city in the world.

For investors

In recent years, Beijing's real estate and automobile industries have continued to develop. The manufacturing industry of Beijing includes automobile industry, biomedicine industry, opto-electromechanical industry, microelectronics industry and so on. In 2019, Beijing's tourism industry received 3.78 million overseas tourists and 310 million domestic tourists.

For professionals

Beijing has a significant influence in the political, economic and trade, cultural, educational and technological fields of mainland China. It is where the headquarters of most state-owned enterprises in China is located, and has the headquarters of the world's largest Fortune Global 500 enterprises. The business center of Beijing gathers a large number of international financial business office facilities and skyscrapers. Zhongguancun area is concentrated in mainland China's important high-tech industries.
